About Our School

Welcome to Golden Child Complex School

Established in 1999, Golden Child Complex School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Ghana. We offer comprehensive education from Creche to Junior High School, focusing on academic excellence, character development, and nurturing each child's unique talents.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment that encourages students to realize their full potential, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that produces well-rounded, innovative, and ethical leaders who positively impact society.


Our Core Values

Academic Programs

Our comprehensive educational programs are designed to nurture students at every stage of their development journey.

Creche

Ages: 6 months - 2 years

A nurturing environment where infants and toddlers receive personalized care while developing essential early skills through play-based learning.

Kindergarten

Ages: 3 - 5 years

A vibrant learning environment where children develop social skills, creativity, and foundational academic concepts through guided play and exploration.

Primary School

Ages: 6 - 11 years

A comprehensive curriculum that builds strong academic foundations in literacy, numeracy, science, and social studies while fostering critical thinking skills.

Junior High School

Ages: 12 - 14 years

An advanced curriculum preparing students for higher education with specialized subjects, research projects, and leadership opportunities.

Admission Process

Join our community of learners with a simple application process.

1

Application

Complete and submit the application form online or in person at our administrative office.

2

Assessment

Schedule an assessment test and interview to determine the appropriate grade level placement.

3

Acceptance

Receive admission decision within two weeks of completing the assessment process.

4

Enrollment

Complete registration by paying fees and submitting required documents to secure your child's place.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Immunization records
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (4)
  • Previous school records (for transfers)
  • Parent/guardian ID (original and photocopy)

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Golden Child Complex School.

Our school hours are from 7:30 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. For Creche and Kindergarten students, there is an option for early pickup at 1:00 PM. Extended care is available until 5:00 PM for an additional fee.

Yes, we offer transportation services to and from school for students living within a 15km radius of the school. Our buses are equipped with safety features and supervised by trained staff. Transportation fees vary based on distance.

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention. Our student-teacher ratios are: Creche (8:1), Kindergarten (12:1), Primary School (15:1), and Junior High School (20:1).

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (football, basketball, swimming), arts (music, dance, drama), clubs (science, robotics, debate), and more. These activities are included in the tuition fees.

Yes, we provide nutritious meals prepared in our school kitchen. Our menu is designed by a nutritionist to ensure balanced nutrition for growing children. We accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies with advance notice.
